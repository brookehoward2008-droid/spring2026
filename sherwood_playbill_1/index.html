The Tale

✦ ✦ ✦

Robbing from the rich has never been so fun! A greedy prince has taken control of England, and it is up to Robin Hood and his band of Merry Men — and women! — to fight for justice and champion the underdog. From comic mastermind Ken Ludwig (Lend Me a Tenor, Crazy for You) comes this rollicking and inventive play, packed with delightful thrills, amorous exploits, contagious laughs, and great beloved heroes like Little John, Friar Tuck, and Maid Marian. So get ready to dodge an arrow or two — you won't want to miss a moment of this swashbuckling adventure!

“Follow the firelight into Sherwood…”
Welcome, brave soul, to the greenwood! Unstring thy worries at the door and follow the sound of the horn into Sherwood. What awaits is a tale of a greedy prince, a stubborn sheriff, a bold outlaw in a hood, and a maid with a far better sword-arm than anyone gives her credit for. There shall be arrows aplenty, kisses stolen, jests hurled, and a great deal of righteous thievery for the common good. Laugh loudly. Gasp freely. Cheer the heroes. Hiss the villains. And if at any point ye feel the urge to shout huzzah — pray, do not hold back.
— The Company
